# Stage 2 — Spec (STUB)

> Spine stage 2: translate a diagnosis into a *generation specification* — what images to synthesize (attributes, counts, conditions) to fix the diagnosed gap.

## What it is
The bridge between diagnosis and synthesis: turning "the model fails on occluded e-bikes at the door under low light" into concrete prompts/conditions/quantities for the generator.

## Why it matters
A good spec is what makes generation *targeted* (the safe regime in [[does-synthetic-data-help]]). It also encodes the synthetic-to-real ratio and the attribute coverage the diagnosis demanded.
